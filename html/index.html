    2023 Detective Pikachu Returns Event

    This event is a special collaborative event to celebrate the launch of the Nintendo Switch title Detective Pikachu Returns.

    Trainers, gear up and join forces with Detective Pikachu to uncover mysteries, capture elusive Pokémon, and immerse yourself in the excitement of the Detective Pikachu Returns Event in Pokémon Go!

    Festival of Lights

    This event was the 2022 annual Festival of Lights event which focuses on Morelull

    Earn and wear the Sparkler clothing set by completing the Festival of Lights Timed Research, adding a festive touch to your avatar's attire.

    Valentine's Day

    To celebrate Valentine's Day, a special event was held focused on various pink Pokémon and introduces Munna and Musharna to the game

    The Valentine's Day 2021 event in Pokémon Go brought trainers together in a celebration of love, friendship, and discovery, enriching their journey with delightful encounters and rewarding experiences.
